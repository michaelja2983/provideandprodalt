Why Provide and Produce

Source Locally

British farmers are now selling some, or all, of the food they lovingly produce straight from their farms? Visit a farm to collect, or order online for delivery to your home.

How?

We don't sell food here - we signpost you straight to the farms that produce it. We help you get closer to the people, places and animals that produce the food you buy and give you the chance to directly support local farmers.

Why

The current food system is failing us and there is an urgent need to reconnect farmers and consumers, if we are to avoid losing great British food and secure the future of traditional family farms.
